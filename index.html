Frequently Asked Questions

What services do we offer?

We offer a range of services including architectural design, interior design, landscape design, and project management for both residential and commercial projects.

How do we charge for your projects?

Fees can vary based on the scope and complexity of the project. Some firms charge a fixed fee, while others may charge an hourly rate or a percentage of the total project cost.

Do you need planning permission for your project?

This depends on the nature of your project and local regulations.

What is the difference between planning permission and building control approval?

Planning permission relates to the use and appearance of the building, while building control approval ensures that the construction meets safety and performance standards.

Do we help with interior design as well?

Yes, we offer comprehensive services that include interior design to ensure that the interior spaces are functional and aesthetically pleasing.

How long does the design process take?

The timeline can vary depending on the project’s complexity. Initial design stages might take a few weeks, while the entire process, including construction, can take several months.

What should I expect during the first consultation?

The first consultation typically involves discussing your needs, preferences, and budget. The designer will also assess the site and provide initial ideas and a fee proposal.
